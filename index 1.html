Crime During SXSW 2015

This Data was taking from the Austin Police Department Incident Report for the 2015 year. I took the data and filtered the dates to show the dates in which SXSW took place this year, which was Mar 13-22. From there I again filtered each crime and took the totals from those crimes to create this pie graph. As you can see form this, theft is the highest crime during SXSW with assaults closely behind.
